Grilled Cheese

Description

The good ol' grilled cheese. Not sure why you're looking up a recipe for this but here you are. Actually I take that back, you can add some sick stuff to grilled cheese to make it taste way better.

This second paragraph is mainly just here to take up space since there is not much to say about grilled cheese. I guess I can use this space to talk about the fact that this recipe contains cheese, so if you're lactose intolerant, you might have a bad time after making and eating this.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices white bread
  • 3 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 2 slices Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Generously butter one side of a slice of bread. Place bread butter-side down in the hot skillet; add 1 slice of cheese. Butter a second slice of bread on one side and place butter-side up on top of cheese.
  2. Cook until lightly browned on one side; flip over and continue cooking until cheese is melted. Repeat with remaining 2 slices of bread, butter, and slice of cheese.
